About

I am a Computer Science and Engineering student based in Dhaka, Bangladesh, with a strong passion for building intelligent systems that solve real-world problems. My work spans Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, and IoT — from designing embedded sensor systems to developing deep learning models for practical applications.
